About Me

Portrait image

Hello, my name is Khoa Nguyen, and I'm a recent graduate from Arizona State University with a major in Computer Science. Born and raised in Mesa, Arizona, I grew up with loving and caring parents and had many opportunities to experience many different things along the way.

My favorite quote is:

If you can't do something, then don't. Focus on what you can do.

This quote is by the character Shiroe from Log Horizon. To give context of this quote, when a person finds themself in a difficult situation where they know that they need to achieve a result but lack the ability to get there, the quote directly states that the individual should place their focus on something, a task or a result, that they are able to achieve.
